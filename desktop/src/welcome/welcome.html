Welcome to Voice2Code

Speech-to-text, right from your menu bar.

Recording

Press Ctrl + Shift + Space to start recording.

Speak naturally — recording auto-stops after 3 seconds of silence, or press the hotkey again to stop manually.

The transcribed text is pasted into whichever app is focused.

Menu Bar

Click the mic icon in the menu bar to access:

  • Start / Stop Recording — same as the hotkey
  • History — view past transcriptions
  • Settings — configure endpoint, API key, audio
  • Test Connection — verify your STT endpoint

Setup

Before your first recording, open Settings and configure:

  1. Your STT endpoint URL (Groq, OpenAI, or self-hosted)
  2. Your API key (if required)
  3. Model and language preferences
